THAT MARRIAGE LYFE

BUILDING STRONGER MARRIAGES

A ministry dedicated to strengthening marriages through faith-based principles and practical relationship tools

"That Marriage Lyfe" is a ministry dedicated to strengthening marriages through faith-based principles and practical relationship tools. We believe that strong marriages build strong families, which in turn create strong communities.

"Therefore a man shall leave his father and his mother and hold fast to his wife, and they shall become one flesh." - Genesis 2:24

OUR APPROACH

At "That Marriage Lyfe," we believe that marriage is a sacred covenant designed by God to reflect His love for His people. Our approach combines biblical wisdom with emotional intelligence strategies, helping couples develop deeper connections, improve communication, and navigate life's challenges together.

We recognize that every marriage is unique, with its own strengths and challenges. That's why we offer a variety of resources, programs, and support systems to meet couples wherever they are in their journey.

Our ministry is built on these core principles:

  • Biblical Foundation: Grounding marriage principles in God's Word
  • Emotional Intelligence: Developing awareness and skills to navigate relationship dynamics
  • Practical Application: Providing tools that can be implemented in daily life
  • Community Support: Creating spaces for couples to connect and grow together

OUR PROGRAMS

Resources and opportunities to strengthen your marriage

Marriage Workshops

Interactive sessions focused on specific aspects of marriage such as communication, conflict resolution, intimacy, and financial management. These workshops provide practical tools and strategies that couples can immediately apply to their relationships.

Learn More

Couples Retreats

Weekend getaways designed to help couples disconnect from daily distractions and reconnect with each other. These retreats combine teaching sessions, guided activities, and private couple time to foster deeper intimacy and understanding.

Learn More

Marriage Mentorship

A program that pairs experienced couples with those seeking guidance and support. Through regular meetings and structured conversations, mentor couples share their wisdom and experiences to help others navigate the challenges of marriage.

Learn More

Date Night Events

Organized events that provide couples with opportunities to have fun together while strengthening their relationship. These events include activities, conversation starters, and sometimes brief teaching moments to enhance connection.

Learn More

Marriage Counseling

Professional support for couples facing significant challenges or seeking to proactively strengthen their relationship. Our network of faith-based counselors provides compassionate guidance rooted in biblical principles.

Learn More

Small Group Studies

Curriculum-based groups where couples can learn, discuss, and grow together in community. These groups provide accountability, support, and opportunities to build friendships with other couples on similar journeys.
