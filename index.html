WHO ARE DR. KOFIE AND DR. LACHELE BRYANT

Dr. Kofie Bryant, H.C. and Dr. Lachele Bryant, H.C. are the visionary leaders behind Inspired Life Ministries (ILM). With a passion for transforming lives through faith-based teaching and community engagement, they have dedicated themselves to helping people discover their purpose and live inspired lives.

As certified emotional intelligence specialists and relationship coaches, the Bryants specialize in helping others optimize their potential while maintaining spiritual and emotional balance. Their approach combines biblical wisdom with practical life applications, creating a holistic ministry experience.

Through ILM and its subsidiaries "That Marriage Lyfe" and "Unbreakable Us," the Bryants are on a mission to strengthen marriages, build communities of faith, and inspire positive change in the lives of all they reach.

UNBREAKABLE US: THE VOW MOVEMENT

"Unbreakable Us" is built on the principle that marriage requires continuous renewal of vows across multiple dimensions of life. The project combines an anthology of real stories with a social movement centered around vow renewal.

Our movement structure (M+M+M=M) includes a powerful Message about overcoming "vow amnesia," Movement components like our #WeTookTheVow campaign, and a Methodology for collecting and sharing transformative stories.

Through retreats, resources, and community support, we help couples create unbreakable bonds by renewing their commitments in areas like love, finances, health, family values, spirituality, conflict resolution, and intimacy.

THAT MARRIAGE LYFE

"That Marriage Lyfe" is a ministry dedicated to strengthening marriages through faith-based principles and practical relationship tools. We believe that strong marriages build strong families, which in turn create strong communities.

Our approach combines biblical wisdom with emotional intelligence strategies, helping couples develop deeper connections, improve communication, and navigate life's challenges together.

Through workshops, counseling resources, and community events, we provide couples with the support they need to thrive in their marriage journey.
