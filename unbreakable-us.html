UNBREAKABLE US: THE VOW MOVEMENT

RENEWING VOWS, STRENGTHENING MARRIAGES

A movement dedicated to helping couples create unbreakable bonds through intentional vow renewal

Unbreakable Us is built on the principle that marriage requires continuous renewal of vows across multiple dimensions of life. Our movement combines powerful messaging, community engagement, and practical methodologies to help couples strengthen their marriages and overcome "vow amnesia."

"What therefore God has joined together, let no man separate." - Mark 10:9

CORE CONCEPT

The Unbreakable Us movement is centered around the concept of "vow amnesia" - the tendency for couples to forget or neglect the commitments they made to each other. We believe that regular, intentional vow renewal across multiple dimensions of life is essential for maintaining a strong, resilient marriage.

Our approach combines an anthology of real stories with a social movement centered around vow renewal. By sharing authentic experiences and providing practical tools, we help couples navigate the challenges of marriage and build unbreakable bonds.

The movement is structured around the formula M+M+M=M:

  • Message: A powerful message about overcoming "vow amnesia"
  • Movement: Community components like our #WeTookTheVow campaign
  • Methodology: Practical approaches for collecting and sharing transformative stories
  • Miracle: The resulting transformation in marriages and relationships

TYPES OF VOWS

Renewing commitments across multiple dimensions of life

Love Vows

Commitments to emotional connection, affection, and prioritizing your relationship.

Financial Vows

Promises regarding financial transparency, shared goals, and responsible stewardship.

Health Vows

Commitments to physical, mental, and emotional well-being for yourself and your partner.

Family Values Vows

Promises regarding parenting, extended family relationships, and household dynamics.

Spiritual Vows

Commitments to spiritual growth, shared faith practices, and moral principles.

Conflict Resolution Vows

Promises regarding communication, forgiveness, and navigating disagreements.

Intimacy Vows

Commitments to nurturing physical and emotional intimacy in your relationship.

MOVEMENT STRUCTURE

M+M+M=M: Message + Movement + Methodology = Miracle

Message Components

  • Anthology Book: Collection of real stories about vow renewal and marriage transformation
  • Teaching Series: Educational content on the importance of vow renewal
  • Devotional Materials: Daily reflections for couples to strengthen their commitment

Movement Components

  • #WeTookTheVow Campaign: Social media initiative encouraging couples to share their vow renewal experiences
  • Vow Renewal Ceremonies: Community events celebrating marriage commitments
  • Marriage Mentorship Program: Connecting experienced couples with those seeking guidance

Methodology Components

  • Vow Renewal Workshops: Practical sessions for crafting meaningful vows
  • Marriage Assessment Tools: Resources for identifying areas needing attention
  • Accountability Partnerships: Support system for couples committed to growth
